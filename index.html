Why Study in Spain?

Studying in Spain can be an enriching and transformative experience for several reasons. Firstly, it's a gateway to a world-class education system. Renowned universities like the Complutense University of Madrid and the University of Barcelona offer diverse programs, many with strong international rankings and research opportunities.

International Recognition

Spain's commitment to the Bologna Process ensures compatibility with European and global educational standards, making your degree internationally recognized.

Study-Work Balance is the Key

About ESC

Eurodream aims to fulfill the desire of Filipinos to have more opportunities not only for studying and working, but also for traveling internationally through the Student Pathway Program in Spain.

With the stamp of approval from Catalunya's renowned Camino Barcelona Centre, we at Eurodream are your one-stop shop for building your dream study abroad experience in Spain. Our expert team will help you find the ideal program at Luis Vives, assist in legalisation filing, and even guide you through securing your Spanish student visa. Don't miss out on this life-changing opportunity - join Eurodream on an unforgettable adventure in Spain!

Services

School Registration

ESC will assist its students with school registration after successfully complying the initial requirements and after settling the required program fees.

Documentation Assistance

Guidelines on permits and legalisation will be given before departure. Prior commencement of their studies, students will be accompanied by our staff in processing their residence declaration,and filing for their Tarjeta de Identidad de Extranjero.

Visa Preparation and Lodging

While it is the student's who will primarily file the visa and attend embassy appointment, ESC will extend assistance on how to request for visa appointment, and give seminar on what to expect and how to answer possible questions that the Immigration Officer will ask.
